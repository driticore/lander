About Lander

Lander is a dedicated non-governmental organization focused on transforming underdeveloped areas in South Africa into thriving, sustainable landscapes. Our mission is to enhance the environment, foster community well-being, and create green spaces that everyone can enjoy.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop landscapes that benefit both people and the environment. We believe in the power of nature to bring communities together, improve quality of life, and promote sustainable living. Through our projects, we aim to restore natural ecosystems, reduce carbon footprints, and provide communities with the resources they need to thrive.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every community in South Africa has access to green spaces that are both functional and beautiful. By focusing on sustainable landscaping practices, we aim to create environments where nature and humanity coexist harmoniously, ensuring a healthier planet for future generations.
